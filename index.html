About ProsTech

At ProsTech, we understand the unique challenges faced by individuals who have experienced limb loss. Our mission is to simplify the process of finding the perfect prosthetic that not only restores functionality but also matches your individual needs and preferences. With our advanced AI and ML algorithms, we have created an intuitive system that takes into account the size and shape of your injury. By using AR camera scanning technology, we accurately assess your missing body parts and recommend the most suitable prosthetics available in our inventory.
